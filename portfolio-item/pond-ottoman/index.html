Pond Ottoman

2019

 

Project Type: Master's Program - Individual Project

Disciplines: furniture concept design, 3D rendering, 3D printing

A peaceful ottoman designed to create a calming feeling like ripples on a pond.

Process

Beginning with simple wooden curves and ending with an interaction inspired by nature. The rings in the center create a calming interaction as they move like the ripples on a pond. The combination of the empty structure with the ripple interaction gives the user a weightless feeling to relax and think.

Final Design and Prototype

To showcase the pond-inspired interaction, I created a 3D printed scale prototype with and small cushion, which showcased the gentle response of the rings when weight was applied.
